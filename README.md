# Religion-Cult-of-the-Duck
🔥 About the Game

Religion: Cult of the Duck is a dark, ironic and atmospheric text strategy game where you build your own cult while trying not to destroy society… or yourself.

You are the leader of a mysterious organization worshipping a strange rubber duck 🦆
At first everything looks like a joke.

Then people start disappearing.
Police become interested.
Followers become fanatics.
And suddenly the conspiracy starts feeling very real.

🎮 Gameplay

Every turn you receive events and must make decisions.

Each choice changes your cult and the world around it.

You need to balance 4 main resources:

Resource	Meaning
🙏 Faith	Power of your cult
🚔 Police	Attention from authorities
👥 Followers	Your believers
💰 Money	Survival of the organization

If one resource reaches the limit — the game ends ⚠️

But survival is not enough.

Your real goal is to uncover the truth behind the cult and reveal the hidden conspiracy of the Duck.
